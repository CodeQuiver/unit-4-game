Crystal Collector!

How to play:

You will be given a random number when the game starts.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

Here's the catch - the value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number at the top, but if your score goes higher than that you lose!

For each new game, the random number and the values of the crystals will change.

Good Luck!
